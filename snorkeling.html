Snorkeling

Description

Departure — Playa Flamingo (7:30AM)
Duration — 4 hours

If you’re excited to explore the underwater world, this snorkeling tour is a fantastic way to do it. You’ll visit rocky reefs teeming with marine life—rays gliding by, colorful angelfish and butterfly fish, and maybe a curious pufferfish or even a reef shark. Keep an eye out for starfish hidden among the rocks—they’re always a fun find!

After a refreshing snorkel, you’ll hop back on the boat to relax and enjoy some tasty snacks. Simple, fun, and a perfect way to experience the ocean’s beauty.

What's Included

  • Two snorkeling excursions at two different sites, with guide
  • Full snorkeling equipment (fins, mask, snorkel, and wetsuit)
  • Snacks & refreshments onboard
  • Transportation (pick up & drop off) - extra fee $10

Frequently asked questions

Do I need any experience to join?

Not at all! Whether you’re a seasoned snorkeler or it’s your first time, this tour is perfect for everyone. The guides are there to help, making sure you feel comfortable and get the most out of your snorkeling adventure.

What can I expect to see underwater?

The waters here are full of life! You’ll likely spot rays, angelfish, butterfly fish, and maybe even a reef shark or pufferfish. Schools of jacks and grunts swim by, and if you’re lucky, you might come across beautiful starfish nestled in the rocks.

What should I bring?

They’ve got your snorkeling gear covered, so just bring along sunscreen, a towel, your bathing suit, and maybe a hat. You’ll want to stay comfortable and protected while soaking up all that sunshine!

Is there Wi-Fi on board?

No Wi-Fi out here, but that’s part of the charm. Disconnect from the world and immerse yourself in the beauty of the ocean. Trust us, you won’t miss it when you’re surrounded by clear water and incredible marine life!

What kind of food is on board?

You’ll enjoy simple, fresh snacks on board—think fresh fruit and some light bites that are perfect after a swim. Just the right amount of fuel to keep you going!

Will there be life vests on board?

Yes, safety is always a priority. There are life vests available for everyone on board, along with other essential safety equipment.

What if it rains?

A little rain won’t ruin the fun, but if the weather turns unsafe, the tour will be canceled. Your safety is the top priority. If that happens, the company will make sure you’re taken care of.

What is the cancellation policy

Please note that we are not responsible for any cancellations, as we are not a tour operator but simply the messengers ;) For information about cancellations, please ask the company directly once we have put you in contact.
