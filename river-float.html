Wildlife river float

& sloth trip

Overview Transportation Similar Tours FAQs Contact

Description

This is the best trip if you want to see as many different animals as possible in one day!

Start your morning floating down the Rio Corobici for about 2 hours, drifting through the Tropical Dry Forest. Keep an eye out for monkeys, lizards, iguanas, birds, and even crocs!

Afterward, enjoy an authentic Costa Rican lunch with several tasty options. Then, head out on a nature walk in the Tropical Rainforest—an easy activity for all ages. You’ll have the chance to spot sloths, frogs, colorful birds, butterflies, and more.

A full day of wildlife and beautiful nature, all in one trip!

What's Included

  • Best trip to see most different animals possible during one single day!
  • Floating on Rio Corobici (Tropical Dry Forest) for about 2hrs in the morning
  • Expected Wildlife during floating: monkeys, lizards, iguanas, birds, crocs
  • Authentic Costarican Lunch (several options)
  • Nature Walk in the Tropical Rainforest (easy activity for all ages!)
  • Expected Wildlife in rainforest: sloths, frogs, birds, butterflies and more
  • Transportation (pick up & drop off)
