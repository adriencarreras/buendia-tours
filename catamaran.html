Catamaran Tour

Overview Reviews Transportation Similar Tours FAQs Contact

Description

Departure — Playa Flamingo
Duration — 4 hours

Sailing along the Guanacaste coast on a catamaran is one of those experiences that just feels like vacation. The boat glides over the waves, the breeze is perfect, and before you know it, you're anchored near a hidden beach. That’s when the real fun begins—snorkeling, kayaking, paddleboarding, or just floating with a drink in hand. Pure relaxation.

Fresh snacks keep you fueled, but the real highlight? Sailing back into one of those unreal Costa Rican sunsets. Picture yourself stretched out on deck, watching the sky turn shades of orange, pink, and gold. And if you’re lucky, you might spot dolphins playing in the waves, rays leaping from the water, or even a humpback whale making an appearance. No two trips are ever the same, and that’s what makes it so special.

Frequently asked questions

What should I bring?

Pack some sunscreen, a towel, and your bathing suit! And don’t forget a hat to shield yourself from the sun. You’ll want to be comfortable and ready for a splashin’ good time.

Is there Wi-Fi on board?

No Wi-Fi here! This is your chance to truly unplug and soak up the beauty of the remote coastline. Leave the distractions behind and immerse yourself in the moment.

What kind of food is on board?

Get ready for some delicious and fresh bites! We’re talking juicy fruit, tasty burritos, and savory dips. Everything is easy to eat and perfect for a day on the water. You’ll be well-fueled for all your fun!

Are life vests provided?

Absolutely! Safety first—life vests are available for everyone on board, so you can relax and enjoy your sail with peace of mind.

What if it rains?

Quick rain showers can be a refreshing part of the experience, especially during the rainy season. However, if the weather becomes unsafe, the tour will be canceled, and you’ll get a full refund. Since we’re not the tour operators, it’s best to contact the catamaran company directly for any weather-related concerns or arrangements.

What is the cancellation policy?

We’re here to connect you with amazing experiences, but we’re not the tour operators ourselves. For details on cancellations, please reach out to the catamaran company directly once we’ve made the introduction.
