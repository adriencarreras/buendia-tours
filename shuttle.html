Airport Shuttle

Getting to and from the airport is simple with private shuttles available for both Liberia Airport and San José Airport. It’s a comfortable and reliable way to travel, so you can start or end your trip stress-free.

Liberia Airport: $120 (1-4 people) + $20 per additional passenger

San José Airport: $350 (1-4 people) + $25 per additional passenger

An extra $20 is charged for late-night pick-ups (between 8:00 PM and 5:00 AM)

If you’re heading somewhere else in Costa Rica, just let us know—we’re happy to help you connect with a great driver or share other transportation options!
