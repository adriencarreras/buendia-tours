Horse Riding

Playa Conchal

Description

Location — Playa Conchal
Duration — 1:30 hours

Exploring the coastline on horseback is such a special way to take in the beauty of the area. The ride along Playa Conchal is especially stunning—soft sand, vibrant blue waters, and lush greenery all around. No wonder it’s considered one of the most beautiful beaches in the world!

Over an hour and a half, you’ll ride along the shore and through shady tree-lined paths—a welcome break from the Costa Rican sun. Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced rider, this tour is open to all. The horses are well cared for, and the guides love sharing this incredible spot. If you’re looking for a peaceful adventure with amazing views, this is the perfect way to spend a morning or afternoon.

90 minutes ride

$65*

This tour is only available for a minimum of 2 people. Price is per person.

*13% VAT tax not included.

Frequently asked questions

How long is the tour?

The ride is about 1.5 hours — just the right amount of time to soak up the beauty of the beach and catch some shade under the trees. Trust us, you’ll love every minute of it!

Do I need to have riding experience?

No worries! This tour is perfect for everyone, whether it’s your first time on a horse or you’ve ridden before. The guides are super friendly and make sure you feel comfortable and confident throughout the ride.

What should I bring?

We recommend bringing sunscreen, bug spray, a cap, and a bottle of water. Long pants are a good idea to avoid any rubbing from the saddle, and closed-toe shoes are better than flip-flops here! Don’t worry about carrying too much, though; there are small bags on the saddle pads for your essentials, so you can leave the backpack behind and focus on the adventure.

Are the horses well taken care of?

Absolutely! The horses are gentle, well-loved, and in great health. Nicole, the owner, takes excellent care of them, so you can relax and enjoy the ride with a happy horse.

What’s the best time of day for the ride?

We love heading out early in the morning or later in the afternoon, when the temperatures are cooler and the lighting makes the scenery even more magical.

What is the cancellation policy?

Please note that we are not responsible for any cancellations, as we are not a tour operator but simply the messengers ;) For information about cancellations, please ask the company directly once we have put you in contact.
